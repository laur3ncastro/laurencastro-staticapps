The Ship's Log

A diary by Capt. Crit McGillicutty

By Crit McGillicutty
Island Breeze Tribune

Where did my sword go?

I put my sword down but I think someone stole it. This sword is so important to me...I don’t know why someone would betray me like that. I’ll have to make them walk the plank.

Also, I got the sword from the octopus guy from Pirates of the Carribean. It’s pretty freaking awesome. He killed so many people with it. That’s sick. I’ll have zero clout if I can’t find it soon.

That’s the other thing,you see. I don’t trust my crewmates. I think they also stole my candy bars from my nightstand. NO ONE should touch those. They’re almost more important than my sword.

It has been a tough week. Choppy water...the works. Hopefully I find my belongings soon.
